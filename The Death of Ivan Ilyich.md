# Chapter 1

Gazette: a journal or newspaper

Ivan Egorovich Shebek: colleague of Ivan Ilych
Fedor Vasilievich: colleague of Ivan Ilych
Peter Ivanowich: colleague of Ivan Ilych

Shebek, Vasilievich and Ivanowich are taking a break from the Melvinski trial in the Law of Court. They are in Ivan's private room debating over whether the Krasovski case is subject to their jurisdiction. Vasilivich states it is not within their jurisdiction (avoiding work), whilst Egorovich thinks it is their responsibility (trying to do work). Ivanovich does not comment upon the matter.
